MoveUp Development-Trek Racing

MoveUp Development-Trek Racing is a young cycling team in the Midwest focused on training the next generation of continental talent for the pro and elite amateur ranks.

This project was a bit of a challenge because the site owner wanted to keep the Wordpress theme that was being used since it was interconnected with his coaching service website. As a result, I had to design within the constraints of what the theme could do…and what I could hack my way around. PHP is like gibberish to me so I was pretty limited with what I could change. Quick side note, trying to hunt down classes in someone else’s WP theme is like trying to find Waldo in a room full Waldos.

The main goals of the site were to feature the team’s sponsors and drive traffic to coaching services on offer with good content marketing such as race recaps, training tips, equipment reviews, etc.
