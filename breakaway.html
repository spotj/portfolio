Breakaway Calculator

The Breakaway Calculator is an app in development for pro cycling race team personnel and cycling fans to accurately estimate the breakaway can hold off the peloton.

If you don't know much about cycling, this might seem like total gibberish so I'll provide a quick rundown why this app is useful for cycling fanatics. In long road races like each day of the Tour de France, it's very common for small groups of riders to get ahead of the main pack of riders (called the "peloton"). If the peloton works together in the right conditions, they can often reel in the breakaway to make sure someone from the pack wins instead. This is a gross oversimplification of bike racing tactics that can be inexplicably complicated at times. Knowning the speed and distance required for the peloton to catch the break is very valuable. This is where the Breakaway Calculator comes in handy.

The two main goals of the site were to feature the team’s sponsors and drive traffic to coaching services being offered. The methods to achieve those goals focused on solid content marketing such as race recaps, training tips, equipment reviews, etc.
